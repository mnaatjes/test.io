# DOs and DON'Ts in Protecting Your Testing Devices Notes

## Importance
Safeguarding devices to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of projects.

## Protection Steps
- Keep devices (USB, laptops) secure.
- Lock computer when not in use.
- Use strong, complex passwords.
- Encrypt confidential files.
- Protect laptop when traveling (do not leave in car/luggage).
- Report lost/stolen devices to Test IO Support and change password.

## ✅ DOs
- Set strong passwords.
- Lock devices when not in use.
- Keep software (OS and browser) up-to-date.
- Restrict access to your computer.
- Be cautious online.

## ❌ DON’Ts
- Don't share passwords or sensitive info.
- Don't leave devices unattended.
- Don't use outdated or unsupported software.
- Don't allow others to access your computer.
- Don't open suspicious emails or click unfamiliar links/attachments.

## ⛔ Prohibited Activities
- Unauthorized P2P (torrent clients).
- Unauthorized sharing of copyrighted material.
- Pornography software or websites.
- Key generators, cracking software, or Wi-Fi hacking tools.
- Anonymizers or software bypassing network monitoring/tracking.
- Outdated or unsupported (vulnerable) software.
- Non-standard encryption tools.
- Cryptocurrency mining or using company resources for profit.
- Hacking software for attacking infrastructure/applications.
