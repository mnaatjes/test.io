# How to keep your device safe for Testing Notes

## Importance
Protecting yourself, customers, and ensuring device availability for testing.

## Data Leakage Prevention
- **Browser Security:** Keep updated; do not save passwords in browser.
- **Passwords:** Use strong, unique passwords; avoid guessable info; enable 2FA; shield device in public.
- **Wi-Fi:** Use WPA3 encryption; use VPN on public Wi-Fi.
- **Encryption:** Enable device encryption for mobile and desktop.
- **Physical Security:** Set lock screen/biometrics; lock computer when not in use; do not leave devices unattended.
- **Backups:** Regular backups to external drives/cloud; verify integrity.

## Virus Protection
- **Antivirus:** Reputable software; keep updated; run regular scans.
- **Software Updates:** Keep OS, apps, and antivirus up-to-date.

## Spam Message/Email Safety
- **Skepticism:** Avoid links/attachments from unknown sources; verify legitimacy.
- **Filters:** Enable spam filters on email accounts.
- **Unsubscribe Trap:** Be cautious; clicking "unsubscribe" in suspicious emails confirms your address to spammers.
- **Mobile:** Customize notification settings to reduce spam exposure.
- **Education:** Stay informed about phishing techniques.

## Additional Tips
- Review app permissions regularly.
- Use encrypted messaging apps.
- Monitor account activity for suspicious transactions.
