## Rules
### Deletion
You can only delete your own report if it has NOT been reviewed by the TL yet.

### Dispute
Available for 'Rejected by Test IO' status.

### Payouts
* **bug_payout:** Triggered at 'Forwarded to customer'.
* **customer_bonus:** 10% additional, triggered at 'Accepted by customer'.


## Important notes
### Prior acceptance
Past acceptance does not guarantee future acceptance. Rules, requirements, or intentional behaviors can change.

### Rejection scenarios
* Customer clarifies behavior is intentional after a previous 'unsure' TL approval.
* Requirements changed but not yet added to 'Known Bugs'.
* Cycle instructions have changed (always read them carefully).
* Dispute manager/TL/CSM specifically asked you to stop submitting that specific issue.
* Overall Test IO rules in the Academy have been updated.
